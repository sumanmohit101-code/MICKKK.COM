Signal-MBI — 6 breadth boxes (4R, 4R-chg, 20R, 20R-chg, 50R, 50R-chg) ka sum, -6 se +6 tak. ≥+3 = Bullish day, ≤-3 = Bearish day, beech me Neutral (ya 3+ red boxes ho par overall red na ho to "Neutral (Warning)").
Net Breadth — (Stocks up 4%+) minus (Stocks down 4%+). Positive matlab buyers hावी.
Net NH-NL — Naye 52-week highs minus naye 52-week lows.
% Above EMA (10/20/50/200) — Kitne % stocks apne respective moving average ke upar trade kar rahe hain.
% Within 15% of 52W High — Kitne % stocks apne 52-week high ke qareeb (85%+) hain.
MBI Trend / Divergence — Aaj ka Signal-MBI 10 din pehle se compare karke "Improving/Weakening" batata hai; Nifty aur MBI opposite direction me jaayein to Divergence flag hota hai.
Table colors — har column apne typical range ke hisaab se red→green shaded hai (jaisa Google Sheet me hai) — jaldi se accha/bura din pehchan sakte ho.
